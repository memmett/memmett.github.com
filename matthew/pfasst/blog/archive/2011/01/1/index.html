Welcome to the PFASST blog. The PFASST blog will highlight new developments, results, and examples of the PFASST technique.

The bird pictured in the top-left corner of this website is a Peregrine Falcon. Peregrine Falcons are really pfasst birds: they can apparently dive at speeds upwards of 320 km/h (200 mph).

The picture was shot at Eagle Heights Wildlife Park (Kent, England), by Keven Law (USA).
