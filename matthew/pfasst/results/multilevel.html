Multilevel PFASST¶

Author:Matthew Emmett
Date:August 19 2011

PyPFASST now supports multiple levels of space/time discretisations. For multi-level runs the levels are traversed in a V-cycle order. For example, during one iteration of a 3-level PFASST run, SDC sweeps are performed on the levels in the following order: 0, 1, 2, 1; and the solution is propagated to the next processor after the sweep on the finest level (level 0).
