Advection/diffusion and viscous Burgers (AD, VB)¶

The AD equation is

\[\partial_t u + v \partial_x u = \nu \partial_{xx} u\]

where \(v\) is some constant velocity and \(\nu\) is the diffusion constant.

The VB equation is

\[\partial_t u + u \partial_x u = \nu \partial_{xx} u.\]

This animation was generated by evolving the initial condition pictured at the beginning of the animation (a Gaussian bump) to a final time of 0.32. The levels are:

  • fine: 512 spatial grid, 9 Gauss-Lobatto SDC nodes; and
  • coarse: 256 spatial grid, 5 Gauss-Lobatto SDC nodes;
  • coarsest: 128 spatial grid, 3 Gauss-Lobatto SDC nodes.

The source code for this example can be found in the examples/advection directory of the PyPFASST repository.
